Take Your Fitness To The Next Level!
-Shaked Toledano Fitness Trainer-

Hi, I'm Shaked!


25 years old from Kfar Yona.
I am a personal and group fitness trainer specializing in weight loss, body sculpting and muscle building for over 7 years.
I also provide nutritional guidance and specialize in functional training, strength training,
kickboxing, Pilates, spinning, Trx, Surfset and have certification for Kangoo Jump training certification.

A little about me:

My fitness journey started at the age of 15 when I realized that I wanted to move beyond dance.
I felt that dancing did not provide me with the health and physical benefits I wanted.
I started working out in gyms and training studios, fell in love with it, lost weight,
got stronger, gained confidence and developed a love for my body. This journey allowed me to discover more about myself.

How I started:

I started training while serving as a combat fitness instructor in the Southern Command and later in the air force's recreational unit.
I did different and amazing training there including trampoline, kickboxing, spinning, pilates, Zumba and more.
During and after the service I decided to turn my passion for fitness into a profession.
I love coaching people, teaching them to love exercise and helping them make their lives the best they can be.

I invite you to join me on this fitness journey
and experience the amazing change it can bring about in your life.
Come train with me and enjoy the satisfaction of an active lifestyle!
