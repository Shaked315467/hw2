Part of the exciting projects I have been involved in includes working with people displaced from their homes due to war. Hostel Collaboration

Once a week, I visit two amazing groups in a hostel for the mentally challenged, and deliver personalized training tailored to their specific needs. I guarantee that they will maintain vital movement and positive activity for their bodies, and offer an escape from the routine for a healthier mind. The satisfaction and energy they give me is worth everything!

24/7 Nutritional Support

This unique project works independently of personal training, Allows participants the flexibility to choose their preferred route (training route/accompanying route) or even combine several routes at the same time. The program spans at least 3 months and ensures the cultivation and learning of sustainable and health-conscious habits. Weekly tests are conducted to monitor weight and, if necessary, adjust the nutrition plan.

(*please note that I am not a certified nutritionist. all recommendations given in this course are based on personal experience and general advice only).
